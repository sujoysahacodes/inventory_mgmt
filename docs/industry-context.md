# Industry Context

## Inventory Management Landscape

### Current Challenges
- **Inventory Visibility**: Limited real-time visibility across distributed locations
- **Demand Forecasting**: Inaccurate predictions leading to overstocking or stockouts
- **Integration Complexity**: Siloed systems with poor data integration
- **Manual Processes**: Heavy reliance on manual tracking and reconciliation
- **Cost Management**: High carrying costs and inefficient capital utilization

### Market Trends
- **Digital Transformation**: Move towards cloud-based, AI-powered solutions
- **Supply Chain Resilience**: Focus on agile and flexible supply chain management
- **Sustainability**: Emphasis on reducing waste and optimizing resource utilization
- **Real-time Analytics**: Demand for instant insights and predictive capabilities
- **Mobile-first**: Need for mobile accessibility and field operations support

### Technology Evolution
- **IoT Integration**: Connected devices for automated tracking
- **AI/ML Applications**: Predictive analytics and intelligent automation
- **Blockchain**: Enhanced traceability and supply chain transparency
- **Cloud-native**: Scalable, microservices-based architectures
- **API Economy**: Seamless integration with ecosystem partners

- Seasonal demand management

- Finished goods management
