# Business Document Requirements (BDR)

## Executive Summary
This document outlines the business requirements for the Inventory Management Accelerator, defining the key business objectives, stakeholder needs, and success criteria.

## Business Objectives

### Primary Objectives
1. **Operational Excellence**
   - Achieve 99.5%+ inventory accuracy
   - Reduce stockout incidents by 80%
   - Improve order fulfillment time by 50%

2. **Cost Optimization**
   - Reduce carrying costs by 25%
   - Minimize obsolete inventory by 40%
   - Optimize procurement spend by 15%

3. **Customer Satisfaction**
   - Improve product availability to 98%+
   - Enhance order accuracy to 99.8%
   - Reduce customer wait times by 60%

## Stakeholder Requirements

### Business Users
- **Inventory Managers**: Real-time visibility and control
- **Procurement Teams**: Automated reordering and supplier management
- **Operations**: Streamlined workflows and exception handling
- **Finance**: Cost tracking and budget optimization
- **Executives**: Performance dashboards and KPI monitoring

### Technical Requirements
- **Integration**: Seamless connection with existing ERP/WMS systems
- **Scalability**: Support for multi-location operations
- **Compliance**: Adherence to industry regulations and standards
- **Security**: Role-based access control and audit trails

## Functional Requirements

### Core Functionality
1. **Inventory Tracking**
   - Multi-location inventory visibility
   - Real-time stock level updates
   - Batch and serial number tracking
   - Cycle counting and adjustments

2. **Demand Planning**
   - Historical data analysis
   - Seasonal trend recognition
   - Forecasting algorithms
   - Demand sensing capabilities

3. **Procurement Management**
   - Automated purchase order generation
   - Supplier performance monitoring
   - Contract management
   - Receiving and quality control

4. **Warehouse Operations**
   - Location management
   - Pick, pack, and ship optimization
   - Cross-docking capabilities
   - Labor management

## Performance Requirements

### System Performance
- **Response Time**: < 2 seconds for standard queries
- **Throughput**: Support 10,000+ concurrent users
- **Availability**: 99.9% uptime SLA
- **Data Accuracy**: Real-time synchronization with < 1% variance

### Business Performance
- **ROI**: Achieve positive ROI within 18 months
- **Efficiency**: 30% improvement in operational efficiency
- **Compliance**: 100% regulatory compliance maintenance
- **Training**: < 2 weeks user adoption time

## Success Criteria

### Quantitative Metrics
- Inventory turnover ratio improvement by 20%
- Reduction in dead stock by 50%
- Improvement in forecast accuracy to 85%+
- Decrease in manual processes by 70%

### Qualitative Measures
- Enhanced user satisfaction scores
- Improved supplier relationships
- Better customer service levels
- Increased operational agility

## Constraints and Assumptions

### Constraints
- Budget limitations and cost considerations
- Integration complexity with legacy systems
- Change management and user adoption challenges
- Compliance and regulatory requirements

### Assumptions
- Stakeholder support and engagement
- Data quality and availability
- Technology infrastructure readiness
- Vendor and supplier cooperation
